Welcome to Bubble Jam!

The bubbles are in a rush! Get popping to clear the traffic jam!

Aim the pointer with your mouse, then click to fire a bubble. If you hit a group of two or more (no matter how far apart), they'll pop!

Gain points for each bubble you pop, lose lots of points for each one that escapes.

Click anywhere to start, and play until you just can't play anymore!
