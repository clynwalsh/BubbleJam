Bubble Jam

The bubbles are late for work! Get popping to clear the traffic jam!

Aim the pointer with your mouse, then click to fire a bubble. If you hit a group of two or more (no matter how widely spaced), they'll pop!

Gain points for each bubble you pop, lose points for each one that makes it to the end of the track.
